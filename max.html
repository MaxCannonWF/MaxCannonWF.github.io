Hi. I'm Max.

I'm a WebBaby in training and we are under RED ALERT.

WebBaby got my life on track after an era of aimless creativity.

After graduating high school in 2011 and working at St. Luke's Hospital by day and the local filmmaking community by night, I helped develop the pitch, pilot, and sizzle reel for what would become HGTV's Boise Boys. Focus was then shifted onto a semi-autobiographical comedy series tiled Banned in Boise that centered on a group of passionate filmmakers trying to retain their indie spirit while competing against a major production company. This project was created, written, pitched, and produced as a PlayStation Original Series pilot for the PlayStation Emerging Filmmakers Program.

Pictured: Max before abandoning his successful hockey career in 2004.

In 2019, the pivot was made from filmmaking into my long-beloved world of interactive media. Working with my fellow WebBabies, I'm currently in the process of shifting my production company, White Font, from a film-focused group into a Boise-based game development studio.
